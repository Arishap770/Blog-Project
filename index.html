My Top Destinations

Tel Aviv, Israel

Tel Aviv is a vibrant Mediterranean city that effortlessly blends sandy beaches, world-class architecture, and a buzzing culinary scene with a rich, modern, and youthful culture. From the ancient port of Jaffa to the neon-lit streets of Florentin, every corner tells a story — and every meal is an adventure.

Photo: Sarona CBD 01 (cropped) by Ynhockey / Wikimedia Commons — CC BY-SA 4.0

Paris, France

Immerse yourself in a world-class blend of iconic landmarks like the Eiffel Tower, timeless art at the Louvre, and exquisite culinary experiences — all set against a backdrop of unparalleled romantic and historic European culture. Paris doesn't just meet expectations; it exceeds every single one.

Photo: La Tour Eiffel vue de la Tour Saint-Jacques by Yann Caradec / Wikimedia Commons — CC BY-SA 2.0

San Diego, California, USA

Visit San Diego for its perfect year-round weather, 70 miles of stunning coastline, and world-renowned attractions like the San Diego Zoo and Balboa Park. A laid-back Southern California culture blends rich history with an incredible food scene — making it one of the most livable and lovable cities on earth.
